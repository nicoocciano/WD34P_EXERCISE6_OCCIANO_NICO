THINGS TO DO IN RIZAL

From the picturesque view of Metro Manilas skyline, its famous pilgrimage spots and interesting cafes, Antipolo City in Rizal remains a charming destination for couples or barkadas who seek a quick escape from the busy city life. Much to the surprise of first-time visitors, Rizal is filled with overlooking restaurants, amazing waterfalls, unique places to visit and even relaxing staycation options!However, While Rizal is quite large area to scope out, you may want to start off in Antipolo city.

CAFE

Any coffee fiend would never tire of finding the best coffee in every city, so if you ever find yourself in Rizal , you're in for a treat. Besides being known for its thriving art scene and its endless stream of outdoor adventures, visitors are sure to find many gems within Rizal's coffee and cafe scene

Best cafe

  • Kaulayaw Coffee

  • The Bistro Pop Up Cafe

  • ArtSector Gallery & Chimney Cafe 360

RESTAURANT

ICYMI, Antipolo is slowly stealing the scene with unbelievably cozy cafe spots in the metro! Rizal for one is beaming with unique tourist spots, relaxing staycation accommodation, and hiking spots to name a few. Whether you're enjoying a road trip with the family, or a date with your special someone, this area is a great option for all kinds of chilling.

WATERFALLS

Get ready to chase some waterfalls in Rizal! There's a lot to look at and forward to in the mountainous region of Rizal. From the wide mountain stretch of Sierra Madre to activities like food trips, and hiking around Antipolo—you're guaranteed to have a memorable time!.

Best WaterFalls

  • Daranak Falls (Tanay, Rizal)

  • Hinulugang Taktak (Antipolo, Rizal)

  • Batlag Falls (Tanay, Rizal)

ATTRACTION

The mountainous province of Rizal is located just at the east of Quezon City. It's a nearby road trip destination for those looking for a quick escape from the city. Whether you want to fill your day with nature through a hike, or take your leisurely time at an art gallery, Rizal has a lot to offer.

Best Attraction

  • Avilon Zoo

  • Angono-Binangonan Petroglyphs

  • ATV Adventures Rizal
